# Privacy Policy - UDP Trigger

**Last Updated:** January 22, 2026

## Overview

UDP Trigger is a low-latency UDP packet sender for Android. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

## Data Collection

**UDP Trigger does NOT collect any personal data.**

The app is designed to operate entirely on your device with no data collection, analytics, or tracking.

## Data Usage

The app sends UDP packets only to destinations (IP addresses and ports) that you explicitly configure. The content of these packets is determined by you.

## Data Storage

All settings and configurations are stored locally on your device using Android's DataStore:

- Destination IP addresses and ports
- Packet content
- Connection presets
- User preferences

No data is transmitted to any server or third-party service.

## Permissions

The app requests the following permissions for functionality only:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| INTERNET | Send UDP packets |
| VIBRATE | Haptic feedback when sending |
| ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE | Auto-reconnect on network changes |
| WAKE_LOCK | Maintain low-latency operation |
| FOREGROUND_SERVICE | Background operation |
| POST_NOTIFICATIONS | Display foreground service notification |

## Third-Party Services

**UDP Trigger does NOT use any third-party services.**

- No analytics platforms
- No advertising
- No cloud services
- No social media integration
- No crash reporting services

## Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. The app does not collect any personal information from users of any age.
